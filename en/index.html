About the Association

An independent union of Kazakhstan's entrepreneurs

The Kazakhstan Entrepreneurs Association is an independent public union representing business interests nationwide — from startups to large holdings.

Our history

The Association was founded by entrepreneurs who came together to systematically protect business, share expertise, and jointly develop the country's business climate.

Goals

Uniting entrepreneurs, attracting investment, developing the entrepreneurial environment and increasing the international competitiveness of Kazakhstani companies.

Tasks

Protecting business interests, expert support, export development, digitalization, educational programs and international cooperation.

Membership benefits

  • Access to 14 specialized departments
  • Priority expert consultations
  • Participation in business missions and forums
  • Access to market analytics and research
  • Representation of interests at the state level
  • Networking with Kazakhstan's business leaders
Mission
“To unite Kazakhstan's entrepreneurs, provide expert support to business, protect the interests of the entrepreneurial community, develop business culture, and promote investment, innovation and the country's sustainable economic development.”
